Port Vila Weather


You can find plenty of information on Port Vila weather here. A seven day Port Vila weather forecasts is provided by World Weather Online.

As local weather is hard to predict other forecasts are available from yr.no the weather service from the Norwegian Meteorological Institute.

Also available is a Port Vila UV Index as well as weather forecasts for beach, walking and water sports activities.
